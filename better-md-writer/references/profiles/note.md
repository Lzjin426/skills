# Profile: Note

## When to use

Use this profile for quick capture, cleanup, meeting notes, internal summaries, and low-friction knowledge capture.

## Reader expectation

The reader wants the point quickly and does not expect a full formal document.

## Default structure

- short opening context if needed
- compact sections only when they help scanning
- short lists for parallel items, actions, or takeaways

## Default carrier

Short paragraphs plus short lists.

## Default passes

- research: `off`
- cognitive-path: `off`
- illustration: `off`
- humanization: `off`

## Style defaults

- keep it brief and direct
- avoid ceremony and over-formatting
- prefer speed of capture over polished exposition
