# Better Md Writer Pass: Research

Research is optional. Default to `off`.

## Levels

- **off**
  - Use when the user already provided the needed material or the document is primarily a record.

- **light**
  - Use for terminology checks, brief validation, or confirming current best practices.
  - Keep the search narrow and purposeful.

- **deep**
  - Use for option comparison, evidence-heavy writing, time-sensitive topics, or when the user explicitly asks for research.
  - Summarize findings, surface conflicts, and cite sources where appropriate.

## When to enable

Enable research only when one of these is true:
- the topic changes over time
- the document compares alternatives
- the reader needs evidence or authority
- the user explicitly asks for research

## Guardrails

- prefer the user's first-hand material when it already answers the task
- do not search just to make a document feel more substantial
- integrate findings into the document's own voice
- flag uncertainty instead of hiding it
